Kiyotaka Ayanokoji

The Enigmatic Protagonist of Classroom of the Elite

About Ayanokoji

Kiyotaka Ayanokoji is the mysterious and highly intelligent main character of Classroom of the Elite. Raised in the secretive and brutal "White Room," he possesses exceptional analytical skills, strategic thinking, and physical ability, but prefers to stay under the radar. His true potential is often hidden beneath a calm and indifferent exterior.

Key Traits & Abilities

  • Extraordinary intellect and strategic planning
  • Mastery of psychological manipulation
  • Highly trained in combat and physical conditioning
  • Calm, observant, and emotionally reserved
  • Tendency to manipulate situations without revealing himself
